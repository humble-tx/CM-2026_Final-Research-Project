# Individual Contribution

The original Pong remake began as a group assignment during Sprint 1. For the final project, I independently extended the concept and transformed it into a practice-based research project.

I developed the narrative structure of the four dream acts, redesigned the gameplay, implemented new interactions in Unity, conducted user testing, and created the physical controller prototypes.

I also explored multiple hardware solutions, including IMU sensors, Bluetooth communication, electromagnets and custom power systems. All testing, revisions and research reflections in this final version were completed independently.
